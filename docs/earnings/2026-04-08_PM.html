  • Margin trajectory: Beer margins are resilient (~38%) but under threat from tariffs on aluminum. Wine & Spirits margins collapsed but this segment is being wound down.

Every company that reported today referenced tariffs — STZ faces 25% aluminum tariffs (direct margin hit), LEVI is absorbing 30% China / 20% ROW tariffs (passing through via pricing so far), and DAL benefits indirectly if fuel prices ease from potential trade de-escalation.   3. AI infrastructure demand remains explosive. APLD's 139% YoY revenue growth and 66% revenue beat confirm that hyperscaler AI capex is flowing to infrastructure providers at an accelerating rate. The 100-MW liquid-cooled data center achievement is a competitive moat.

  4. Premium travel demand is resilient but fuel is the wildcard. DAL's record revenue and premium seat mix shift show consumer demand for experiences remains strong, but $4.30/gal fuel creates a binary earnings outcome — either management successfully passes through costs or margins compress sharply.

  5. Operational improvement stories can deliver outsized surprises. RPM's 63% EPS beat with record sales across all three segments shows that companies executing on cost optimization can materially outperform even in a challenging macro.
